About C.P.H.

Founded on September 15, 2025, Chiggas Production House is a creative powerhouse dedicated to pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling. We are a collective of passionate filmmakers, artists, and innovators who believe in the power of a well-told story.

From concept to final cut, we handle every aspect of production with meticulous care and a commitment to excellence. Our mission is to create unforgettable experiences that resonate with audiences worldwide.

Our Services

Pre-Production

Concept development, scriptwriting, storyboarding, and casting. We lay the perfect foundation for your project.

Production

State-of-the-art filming, directing, cinematography, and sound recording with our expert crew and equipment.

Post-Production

Editing, color grading, visual effects, sound design, and final delivery. We polish your story to perfection.
